Are there restrictions on what I can use the divorce loan for?

No. You can use the funds at your own discretion whether it is for legal costs, financial commitments and/or personal expenses.

What do you need from me to approve my divorce loan?

After you submit your application, we will contact you to review a few items. Typically, we will request your Separation Agreement, Divorce Application and Response, and sworn Financial Statements. Other information may be required depending on your situation, but we will make the process as easy and stress-free as possible.

Do you require a credit check?

We usually do not require a credit check.

What different ways can my divorce loan be set up?

We can set up your divorce loan as a lump sum payment, staged over a period of time or as a open line of credit. We will review each option with you so you can decide which will work best for your situation.

What happens if I need more money?

You can apply for another loan with us at any time. We will discuss available options with you should you require additional funds.

When do I have to pay back my divorce loan?

Repayment is not required until your divorce settlement or equalization payment has been finalized no matter how long that takes, including the sale of the matrimonial home (or any other assets) and your lawyer is in receipt of the funds. You can repay the entire loan or set up a payment plan at any point before the divorce is finalized without any penalty or fees.
